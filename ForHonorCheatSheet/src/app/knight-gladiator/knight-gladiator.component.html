Gladiator Moveset

Hero Specific
Renown
Earn more Renown in 1v1 fights, by killing enemy Heroes, and getting killing streaks to unlcok your feats in a match.
Revenge Mode
Boosts Damage and Health. All Attacks are Uninterruptible.
Parry and Throws knock enemies down.
Attacks are Auto-Parried on activation.
Defense: Reflex
Your Guard Stance only remains active for a limited time.
Improved Stamina
Larger stamina meter and faster regeneration.
Reduces cool down time.
Deflect
Deflect by dodging in the direction of an incoming attack just before impact.
Deflect can be followed up by Skewer.
Zone Attack
Cancel Zone Attack after the first Attack with B.
Bamboozle
Press X after missing your sprint attack, to throw a punch that Stuns the opponent.
Special Fuscina Ictus
Use your Fuscina Ictus on an enemy that's out of Stamina to unbalance them.
Skewer
The longer the Skewer lasts, the greater the Bleed damage.
Against a wall, press X to Stun the opponent.
Press A or B to exit.
Skewer Throw
You can throw your opponent while they are Skewered.
Not effective after Bleed has been applied three times.
Chains: Skewer and Fuscina Ictus
Must be used to end a chain or at the end of a chain.
