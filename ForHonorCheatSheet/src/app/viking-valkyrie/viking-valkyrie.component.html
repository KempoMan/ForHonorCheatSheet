Valkyrie Moveset

Hero Specific
Renown
Renown is balanced across activities.
Revenge Mode
Boosts Damage and Health. All Attacks are Uninterruptible.
Parry and Throws knock enemies down.
Attacks are Auto-Parried on activation.
Superior Block Light Attacks
Light Attacks have Superior Block property during the Startup. Use it to counter attacks.
Dodge Counter
Your Guard automatically matches the direction of your Dodge, Pouncing Thrust and Hunter's Strike. Press RT after a successful Dodge Block to link into a Shoulder Pin.
Full Block Stance on Shield Tackle
Hold Shield Tackle to gain Full Block Stance. It keeps full block property during the charge as well.
Shield Tackle Cancel
Press RB to Cancel Shield Tackle with Pouncing Thrust.
Press X to Cancel Shield Tackle with a Guardbreak.
Shield Crush Cancel
Press X during a Heavy Attack Startup to Cancel into a Shield Crush.
Spear Sweep Finisher
Use Spear Sweep instead of your normal Chain Finisher to surprise your enemy.
Zone Attack
Cancel Zone Attack after the first attack with B.
