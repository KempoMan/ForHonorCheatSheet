Highlander Moveset

Hero Specific
Renown
Renown is balanced across activities.
Revenge Mode
Boosts Damage and Health. All Attacks are Uninterruptible.
Parry and Throws knock enemies down.
Attacks are Auto-Parried on activation.
Offensive Form and Defensive Form
Highlander's Guard Mode has two forms:
Defensive Form has access to Block, Parry and Guardbreak.
Offensive Form has access to Unblockable Attacks
Entering Offensive Form
Hold RT to enter Offensive Form. Continue to hold RT to remain in Offensive Form.
Offensive Form Unblockable Heavy
When in Offensive Form, Release RT for an Unblockable Heavy Attack. After this attack you will return to Defensive Form.
Fast Flow to Offensive Form
Hold RT after a Parry, Guardbreak Throw, or after any Attack other than Defensive Form's Chain Finisher.
Exiting Offensive Form
Press B to return to Defensive Form. Suffering a hit will also cause you to return to Defensive Form. After an Attack, you return to Defensive Form if you do not hold RT.
Offensive Form Enhanced Light Attack
Offensive Form light is not interrupted when blocked. Offensive Form Light can be used to Cancel an Offensive Form Unblockable Heavy.
Defensive Form Heavy Attacks
Heavy Attacks have Super Armor.
Defensive Form Light Openers
Light Opener Attacks have Superior Block.
Zone Attack
Cancel Zone Attack after the first or second Attack with B. Cannot fast flow to Offensive Form.
Sprint Attack
Cancel Sprint Attack after the first Attack with B. Cannot fast flow to Offensive Form.
Offensive Form Special Cancels
Unblockable Heavy can be Cancelled into Fomorian Kick.
Unblockable Heavy can be Cancelled into Caber Toss.
Kick can be Cancelled into Caber Toss.
(CCC) Celtic Curse Cancels
Cancel in a Left Attack with LS + RT
Cancel into a Right Attack with LS + RT
These Attacks have Super Armor.
Celtic Curse Chain Starter
Celtic Curse, and the side versions it can Cancel into, can be used to initiate Chains.
