Raider Moveset

Hero Specific
Renown
Earn more Renown in outnumbered fights, by controlling objectives, and by killing enemy soldiers to unlock your feats in a match.
Revenge Mode
Boosts Damage and Health. All Attacks are Uninterruptible.
Parry and Throws knock enemies down.
Attacks are Auto-Parried on activation.
Stampede Charge
Hold/Release X to manage Sprint distance.
Knocks down enemies while running.
Stuns Grabbed opponent if knocked into wall.
Controlled Throw
Hold/Release X to manage where you Throw enemy.
Opponent loses Stamina each time he knocks a nearby character.
Raider Fury
Attack is Unblockable and can be used with any chain.
Stunning Tap Variation
Cancel any Heavy Attack startup with a Light Attack.
Stun opponent.
Moves (From Guard Mode) LT
Chain Chop and Poke RB, RB, RB
Chain Wild Swinging RT, RT, RT
Chain Winding Slashes RT, RB, RT
Unblockable Raider Fury RB + RT
Unblockable Knockdown Stun Stampede Charge Not in Guard Mode L3 + Hold X
Reaping Charge Not in Guard Mode L3 + RT
Stun Stunning Tap LS or LS or LS + A, RB
Stun Rushing Guardbreak LS or LS or LS + A, X
